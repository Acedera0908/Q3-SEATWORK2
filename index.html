When Marnie Was There



CHARACTER IMAGE CHARACTER DESCRIPTION

Marnie

Marnie was the "person" that Anna had spent the most time with while she was in Kissakibetsu. Spoiler alert: In the ending of the film, it is revealed that Marnie is actually Anna's grandmother.

Anna

Anna had suffered from asthma during the beginning of the story, so she was sent to Kissakibetsu to live with her adoptive mother's siblings. While there, she meets a girl named Marnie. These two spent a lot of time together, near the Marsh House where Marnie lived. However, the thing is, in one day, Anna sees that the House is occupied, but the next, it is not. In the end, we might say that Anna was "hallucinating" everything that she experienced in Kissakibetsu.

Sayaka

Sayaka is a young girl that moves into the old Marsh House. She tells Anna about the journal that Marnie used to have, and develops a friendship with the former.

Setsu and Kiyomasa Oiwa

Setsu and Kiyomasa Oiwa are married. Setsu is the older sister of Anna's adoptive mother, Yoriko. They are the ones who took care of Anna whilst she was in Kissakibetsu.

Yoriko

Yoriko is Anna's adoptive mother. She is very caring towards Anna. When the doctor suggested that Anna be sent to somewhere with good weather in order to help her asthma, she allowed Anna to stay there. She and her husband are in no way biologically related to Anna.
